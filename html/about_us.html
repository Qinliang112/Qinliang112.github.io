ABOUT US

IKEAN is the market leader in designing and supplying the furniture. We make furniture that comforms to safety requirement formulated by government. We focus wide range of furniture. Thousands of IKEAN furniture are used throughout Malaysia and supported by outstanding customer service.

At Ikean, we understand the importance of sustainablilty and ensure their safety during usage. So, we have do many research and experiment every stage from design to assembly. The performance of our quality system is continually reviewed through monitoring and measurement of company performance indicators and regular management reviews. We commited to reduce our impact ot environment. To do so, we have implemented many environmental effort such as minimizing waste, using renewable materials and encouraging our customer to operate in the sensitive environmental way.

Apart from that, we also pay attention to the working environment. Every employees is relaxed when they are working in our working environment. We can pride that there is no problem regarding our environment exists in our company. Besides that, we also do many survey to confirm the common price for different type of furniture to make sure everyone can afford our furniture. Sometimes, we will do some promotion for certain type of furniture. Customer can pay attention to our website.
