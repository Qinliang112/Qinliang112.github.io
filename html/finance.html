Finance options

If you've found out how you want your dream home, but need some help spreading the cost, we can offer you a range of finance options to help you make your ideas a reality.

Instalment Payment Plan (IPP)

[ 0% interest. 100% interested? ]

We have flexible payment options from as low as RM500 to make it easy & affordable to get the home you dream of.

FOR IN-STORE PURCHASES:

Exclusively for RHB Bank, Maybank, Maybank American Express, CIMB, Hong Leong Bank, HSBC, UOB and Affin Bank credit card holders.


  • 6 months for a minimum RM500. Valid for all participating banks.
  • 12 months for a minimum RM1000. Valid for all participating banks EXCEPT for UOB (minimum RM500).
  • 24 months for a minimum RM1000. Valid for all participating banks.

FOR ONLINE PURCHASES:

At the moment, we offer tenure of 6,12 and 24 months only for Maybank credit card holders. Select to pay with our Instalment Payment Plan at check-out.
