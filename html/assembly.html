Assembly

Your new furniture deserves a striking stage. Looking to install or repair a light fixture? Planning a painting project?


Considering a full kitchen or bathroom remodel? Contact your local IKEAN Store to see what other renovation or repair services our professionals can provide for your home or business in tandem with the assembly and installation of your IKEAN purchase.

Our products are specifically designed to be simple to assemble so that our customers can easily do it themselves. That way, you save the most money.


However, if you need a hand, we have a range of assembly options for you to choose from when shopping at IKEAN.my or shopping at your nearest IKEAN store.


We are currently offering assembly services for customers in accordance to the updated SOPs issued by the Government. We would like to reassure that all our professional assemblers are fully vaccinated in accordance to the National Security Council of Malaysia directives. We would also require our customers who will be in contact with our assemblers to be fully vaccinated as well.
